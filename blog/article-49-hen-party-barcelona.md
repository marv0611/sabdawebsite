## The 10 best hen party ideas in Barcelona

### 1. Private immersive session at SABDA
Book the 360° projection room at [SABDA](https://sabdastudio.com/hire/) for a private group session: breathwork + sound healing + ice bath on the patio. 20-80 people. The visuals can be customised. Nobody in the hen party will have done anything like it before. From €350/session. [connect@sabdastudio.com](mailto:connect@sabdastudio.com).

### 2. Vermouth crawl in Gràcia
Four stops, four vermouths, four different tapas. Vermuteria del Tano → La Vermu → Bodega Quimet → Bar Canigó. ~€20-25/person. No sashes required.

### 3. Sailing with cava
Private sailboat for the group. 2-3 hours along the coast, cava on board, swimming stop. ~€50-80/person depending on boat size.

### 4. Cooking class in a secret garden
Local sisters Clara and Sara teach paella with wine and rooftop views. ~€60/person. Groups up to 12.

### 5. Spa at Aire Ancient Baths
Stone vaults, candles, thermal pools in the Born. ~€80-100/person for spa + massage. Book ahead for groups.

### 6. Group ice bath (yes, really)
If your group is the "we do weird things and laugh about it" type, [SABDA's ice bath](https://sabdastudio.com/classes/ice-bath/) is perfect. Guided breathwork + cold plunge. Everyone emerges laughing, shaking, and feeling ridiculously alive. From €16/person.

### 7. Bunkers del Carmel at sunset
Best free viewpoint in Barcelona. 360° panorama. Perfect for the group photo. Bring drinks.

### 8. Chocolate workshop
Make your own truffles and bars at Chök or ChocoMuseum. From €25/person.

### 9. Intimate flamenco
Tarantos (Plaça Reial) or Tablao de Carmen (Poble Espanyol). Small rooms, real dancers. From €25 with a drink. Goosebumps guaranteed.

### 10. Private terrace party
Rent a terrace in Eixample or Gràcia via Airbnb or Giggster (€200-400). Your music, your catering, your rules.

## The perfect hen party combo

**Option A — Wellness + food:** Morning: private SABDA session → Midday: brunch in Eixample → Afternoon: vermouth crawl in Gràcia.

**Option B — Adventure + party:** Morning: sailing with cava → Afternoon: cooking class → Evening: dinner + Sant Antoni bars.

Either comes in under €150/person for a full day the bride will remember far longer than a limo ride.
