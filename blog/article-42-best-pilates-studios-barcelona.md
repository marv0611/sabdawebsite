Barcelona's pilates scene is dense. Reformer studios, mat studios, gym chains with pilates classes, and one studio where you do pilates inside a 360° projection room. Here's the honest comparison.

---

## 1. SABDA — Immersive mat pilates
[SABDA](https://sabdastudio.com/classes/) offers mat pilates inside a 360° room with Dolby Atmos spatial audio. The environment captures your focus in a way mirrors and background music can't. Formats: Core Pilates, Power Pilates, Pilates Sculpt, Full Body Pilates.

**Type:** Mat (immersive) | **Price:** From €16/class | [3 for €48](https://momence.com/m/443935)
**Location:** C/Muntaner 83B, Eixample | **Best for:** Focus-amplified mat work

## 2. Dona10 — The complete reformer centre
Reformer, mat, Cadillac, Wunda Chair. The most complete pilates equipment setup in Barcelona. Professional instructors, prenatal programme, beautiful facilities.

**Type:** Reformer + mat | **Price:** ~€25-35/class group, ~€45-60/individual
**Location:** Eixample | **Best for:** Full-equipment pilates, rehabilitation

## 3. Pilat3s — Technical reformer
Small-group reformer (6-8 people) with a technical focus. Popular with physiotherapists for post-injury work. Individual sessions with postural assessment available.

**Type:** Reformer | **Price:** ~€25-30/class, ~€50-65/individual
**Location:** Eixample | **Best for:** Rehab, technique-focused

## 4. Simply Be Pilates — Gràcia intimate
Reformer and mat in a cozy Gràcia space. Small groups, well-trained teachers, neighbourhood feel.

**Type:** Reformer + mat | **Price:** ~€20-28/class
**Location:** Gràcia | **Best for:** Intimate, personal attention

## 5. Holmes Place / DIR — Gym-included pilates
Major gym chains include pilates mat and sometimes reformer in their membership. Quality depends on the teacher and location. Large group classes (15-20 people for mat).

**Type:** Mat (+ reformer at some locations) | **Price:** Included in membership (~€60-90/mo)
**Best for:** People who already have a gym membership

## 6. PilatesBCN — Dedicated reformer
Reformer-focused studio with structured programmes and small groups. Clean, functional space.

**Type:** Reformer | **Price:** ~€25-30/class
**Location:** Eixample | **Best for:** Committed reformer practitioners
