Alpro · Danone

Cherry Flavour Launch

Alpro chose SABDA to unveil their new cherry flavour to press and influencers. We created custom 360° branded visuals that transformed the immersive room into a living product experience — projections synced to music, Dolby Atmos spatial audio, and a fully branded environment from entrance to bar.

FC Barcelona

Sergi Roberto Farewell

FC Barcelona filmed Sergi Roberto's official farewell video inside SABDA's immersive room. Our projection mapping created the backdrop for one of the club's most emotional tributes — a cinematic experience that blended the player's career highlights with real-time 360° visuals.
