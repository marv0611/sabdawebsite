Immersive Pilates

Pilates at SABDA isn't what you'd expect from a studio in Barcelona's Eixample district. The room disappears. If you're looking for a centro de pilates in Barcelona that goes beyond the ordinary, this is it. 360° laser projections surround you on every surface. Dolby Atmos spatial audio fills the space from invisible in-wall speakers and ceiling subwoofers. And then you move. Mat pilates, all levels, props provided. The projections create focus. The audio creates rhythm. You don't think about your phone, your inbox, or the noise on Muntaner outside. You just move.

We run both Power Pilates and Core Pilates. Power is faster, full-body, with cardio elements built into the flow. Core is slower and more precise, targeting deep abdominal engagement, spinal alignment, and controlled breathing. Both take place inside the immersive room with custom visuals designed for each class. This isn't a screen on the wall. This is the room itself becoming part of the practice.

What to Expect

All sessions are mat pilates, not reformer. We don't use reformer equipment. Instead, the 360° immersive room itself becomes the tool that transforms your practice. Classes run 50 minutes. Arrive 10 minutes early to set up your mat and settle in. We provide mats, blocks, bands, and all props you need. Wear comfortable, form-fitting clothing. Pilates is done barefoot or in grip socks. Classes are taught in English with Spanish cues where helpful. Changing rooms and showers are available.

  • 50 minute sessions, all levels
  • Mat, blocks, and bands provided
  • Barefoot or grip socks
  • Changing rooms and showers available
  • English with Spanish cues

Why Immersive Pilates

The question people always ask: is this just regular pilates with a screen in the corner? No. The projections wrap around the entire room, floor to ceiling, wall to wall. The Dolby Atmos audio doesn't come from a speaker on the floor. It moves around you, above you, through you. The effect isn't a gimmick. It creates a kind of focus that most people have never experienced in a group fitness class.

The visuals are custom designed for each pilates format. Power Pilates might run through geometric landscapes that pulse with the tempo of the class. Core Pilates might dissolve into oceanic slow motion, matching the precision of the movement. The room responds to the energy of the session. That's the difference between immersive pilates at SABDA and a regular mat class anywhere else in Barcelona.
