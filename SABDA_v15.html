What is SABDA

Not a yoga studio.
Not a museum.
Something new.

SABDA is Barcelona's first immersive audio-visual wellness studio — a space where art, technology, and movement converge. Walk in from the streets of Eixample and step into 360° laser projections, cinema-grade spatial audio, and classes that move your body and your mind.

Every class is a custom-designed world. Vinyasa through abstract landscapes. Sound healing inside living geometry. Breathwork under infinite starfields.
