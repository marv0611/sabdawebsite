What is SABDA

A Wellness Studio
Built Different.

SABDA is an immersive wellness studio in Barcelona's Eixample district. 360° laser projections on every surface. Spatial audio from invisible in-wall speakers. Yoga, pilates, sound healing, breathwork, ecstatic dance, and ice bath, each inside a different immersive world.

Every class transforms the room. Vinyasa through abstract landscapes. Sound healing inside living geometry. Breathwork under infinite starfields. C/Muntaner 83B, Eixample. 10 minutes from Universitat metro.

SABDA is an immersive audio-visual wellness studio in Barcelona's Eixample district. We offer yoga, pilates, sound healing, breathwork, meditation, ecstatic dance, and ice bath inside a 360° projection room with Dolby Atmos spatial audio. IDEAL, Moco Museum, and IKONO are immersive art exhibitions where you walk through and observe. SABDA is an immersive wellness studio where you actively participate. You move, breathe, and practice yoga, pilates, sound healing, or breathwork inside 360° laser projections with Dolby Atmos spatial audio. It's the difference between watching a concert on a screen and being inside the music.
