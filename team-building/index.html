Corporate Wellness & Team Building

Why companies choose SABDA.

SABDA is an immersive audio-visual venue in Barcelona's Eixample district with 360° laser projections and cinema-grade Dolby Atmos spatial audio. Companies come here for corporate wellness, team experiences, and workshops that go beyond the usual. 360° projections and spatial audio create an environment where people connect through shared experience.

We've hosted brand activations for Alpro and Danone, filmed a farewell video for FC Barcelona's Sergi Roberto that hit 22 million views, run immersive wellness workshops for corporate teams, and built custom branded worlds for product launches. Whether your team is 15 or 150, we design experiences around what you need.

Experience Formats

Immersive Wellness Workshop
