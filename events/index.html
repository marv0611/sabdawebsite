Exhibition

Panta Rhei

An immersive exhibition that brought the ocean’s changing seasons to life through art, science, and technology.

The first of four seasonal exhibitions, Panta Rhei unfolded in two parts: an educational interactive zone exploring real ocean data and climate visualizations, and The Portal, a meditative 360° audiovisual space where sound and imagery responded in real time to live ocean data.

Beyond the weekly class schedule, SABDA runs a rotating programme of events: exhibitions (digital artists take over the 360° room), ecstatic dance, listening sessions in Dolby Atmos, ceremonies built around breathwork and sound, and community events. Events are announced through our Instagram and newsletter.

Yes. The immersive room holds up to 100 guests standing and is available for full private hire. Every celebration is yours for the duration of the event.
