The Immersive Experience

An experience that doesn't exist
anywhere else.

SABDA is an immersive space in Barcelona's Eixample district. An 86m² room with 360° laser projections across every surface and a certified Dolby Atmos spatial audio system. Not a museum. Not a gym. A space where you participate, move, breathe, and feel inside the art.

If you're looking for immersive activities in Barcelona, you've probably seen IDEAL, IKONO, or Moco Museum. The difference is that at SABDA, you don't watch. You're part of the experience.

Immersive Exhibitions

Audiovisual exhibitions by digital artists inside our 360° room. Panta Rhei was our first immersive exhibition: a generative audiovisual work where the audience lay surrounded by projections and Dolby Atmos spatial sound. Each exhibition transforms the space into an entirely different sensory experience.

Listening Sessions

Immersive listening sessions where the room becomes the inside of the music. Lying on the floor, surrounded by visual projections that respond to the sound, you hear a curated set through Dolby Atmos spatial audio. Artists like Jon Hopkins, Max Cooper, Bicep, and Brian Eno take on an entirely new dimension in this format.

Immersive Classes

Over 43 weekly classes of yoga, pilates, sound healing, breathwork, and ecstatic dance inside the 360° room. Each class has its own projections: abstract landscapes for vinyasa, infinite skies for breathwork, visual frequencies for sound healing. All levels. Equipment provided. See schedule.

Private Events and Activations

The 360° room can be fully customised for your brand, event, or celebration. FC Barcelona, Alpro, Danone, Decathlon, and Elisava have already trusted SABDA. From brand activations to audiovisual productions, corporate team building, and immersive dinners. More information and pricing.
